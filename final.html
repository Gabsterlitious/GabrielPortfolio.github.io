Final Project

Description: The final project was a game that we did at the end of the class to showcase how far we've gotten. I made a game where there are blue and red balls on the screen and you have to get each ball to the correct side of the field. The player can move the hole in the wall to try and get them on the correct sides, and for the last level, they have to click the screen to open the portal to the other side.
